About Me

Hello. My name is Daniel Klotz and I am an aspiring developer. Born in a small village in Austria, I am currently living in Leipzig, Germany. My curiosity for programming has started forming very early in life as the son of a C++ software engineer. Getting my first computer at the age of 9, and internet access shortly thereafter, set me up to discover many interesting things. After spending most of my youth and young adulthood pursuing other things, in 2017 I chose to develop my interest in computer science into a career.

So far, my journey has been very exciting. There is a huge difference in perspective from where I started to where I am now, as this field is way deeper than I anticipated. This has only strengthened my motivation; I am very enthusiastic about the future and ready to learn. As I am entirely self-taught, I have fallen into books and docs more often than I care to admit. It is hard to find a clear structure - to maximize ones efficiency in learning while keeping ones curiosity satisfied. Which is exactly why I am applying for junior development positions. The ability to get a first-hand experience of corporate workflow, combined with the opportunity to personally learn from bright minds, is invaluable.
